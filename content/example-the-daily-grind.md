# Why I love this spot

This cafe has the best lighting for working and incredible espresso. The baristas
remember regulars by name, which is a small thing that goes a long way.

- **Top Pick:** Oat milk latte
- **Vibe:** Quiet & studious
- **Best time:** Weekday mornings before 10am
